Global Sales by Genre and Platform

In terms of platform sales, PS2, released in 1994, had the all-time best global sales between 1980-2020. Other consoles that dominate the sales are the Xbox 360, Wii, DS, and PS. These platforms had doubled to tripled the amount of global sales compared to other consoles. As for genres, action, sports, and shooters appear to be the most popular. However, genre sales depend on when a platform was released. We can see that in older consoles, shooter games were not as common, but on new consoles like the Xbox 360, PS3, and PS4, they have skyrocketed.
